Apple Intelligence, first.

Chat with Apple Intelligence running entirely on your device. Your messages never leave your iPhone. No account, no subscription, no telemetry.

On-device by default

The default chat mode uses Apple Intelligence through the FoundationModels framework. Responses are generated locally on your iPhone, with zero network traffic.

More open-source AI models

Additional open-source AI backends are available as alternative options. The list of supported models will keep expanding over time.

Three ways to start

Sign in with Apple for private on-device chats synced via iCloud, use phone sign-in, or start anonymously in a single tap.

iOS 26 Liquid Glass

Built entirely in SwiftUI using Apple's new Liquid Glass design language. Glass buttons, ambient backdrops, frosted sheets, and smooth, playful motion.

Zero collection, zero ads

No analytics, no trackers, no ads, and no data collected by the developer. Authentication tokens stay in the iOS Keychain on your device.

Apache 2.0 open source

Free, non-commercial, and fully open-source. Fork it, audit it, extend it — the client is built from public code, by design.

Built to be trusted.

AI Open Client is a free, non-commercial iOS client for public neural networks. It has no third-party UI dependencies, no analytics SDKs, and no hidden network calls. The code is open for anyone to read.
